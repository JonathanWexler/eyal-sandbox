The Firm's Vision:

Sharon Law, P.A. is a boutique firm dedicated to advise and advance its clients' corporate governance, business management, business transactions, and real estate deals.

Sharon Law, also serves as outside general counsel for business clients who do not have the budget to pay in-house legal counsel but nonetheless seeks general counsel advice.

And More:

The Firm's Attorney:

Mr. Eyal Sharon, Esq., is the founder and managing partner of Sharon Law, P.A. With several years of corporate and business consulting experience, as well as corporate defense in complex litigation, Mr. Sharon has the unique capability to advise clients regarding corporate structures, business operations, commercial and real estate transactions and methods of minimizing litigation risks.

With licenses in Florida and New York, Mr. Sharon is able to promote its client's business interests in two of the largest economic hubs in the United States.
